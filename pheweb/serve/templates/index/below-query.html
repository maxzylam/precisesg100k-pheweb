Welcome to PheWeb

PheWeb is a tool for exploring phenotype-genotype associations. You can search for specific variants, genes, or phenotypes to discover relationships between genetic variations and observable traits.

How to Use This Page

  • Enter a variant ID (e.g., rs12345) to find phenotype associations
  • Search for a gene name (e.g., BRCA1) to discover related variants and phenotypes
  • Look up a phenotype (e.g., height, blood pressure) to explore genetic associations
  • Browse curated phenotypes using the "Phenotypes" tab at the top
  • Check "Top Hits" to view the most significant associations in our database

Example Searches

  • rs9939609 (FTO variant associated with BMI)
  • APOE (gene associated with Alzheimer's)
  • Type 2 diabetes (complex disease trait)

Data Sources

Our database integrates results from multiple genome-wide association studies (GWAS), providing a comprehensive resource for exploring genetic associations across diverse populations and phenotypes.
