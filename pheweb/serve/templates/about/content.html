About This PheWeb

PheWeb is an interactive web platform designed for exploring and visualizing genotype-phenotype associations from large-scale genomic studies. This instance is maintained by the PRECISE Singapore 100K project, providing researchers and clinicians with a powerful tool to investigate genetic variants and their associations with various traits and conditions.

This site was built with PheWeb version 1.3.16.

All positions are on GRCh37.

PRECISE Singapore 100K

The Precision Health Research Singapore (PRECISE) 100K project aims to enable a new level of healthcare by analyzing genetic and clinical data from 100,000 Singaporean volunteers.

This initiative helps accelerate scientific discoveries, develop new treatments, and create predictive models to improve prevention and management of diseases relevant to Singapore's multi-ethnic population.

Data Sources

This database integrates results from comprehensive genomic analyses of Singaporean populations, including:

  • Genome-wide association studies (GWAS) from Singaporean cohorts
  • Whole genome sequencing data from the PRECISE 100K project
  • Curated phenotype data from electronic health records
  • Integrated data from relevant international genetic databases
